Snapdeal.com partners with Hero MotoCorp to sell two-wheelers through its platform: Snapdeal claims that it saw 700 two-wheelers being sold within 72 hours of launching the new category a week ago. The vehicles are priced as same as offline dealerships. For Snapdeal, automobile is one of the most promising categories, and it expects it to reach Rs 1,000 crores in GMV in the near term.
