Mary Meeker's 2015 Report is Out, and Calls Out that India Tech is at an Inflection Point: Kleiner Perkins partner Mary Meeker s yearly Internet Trends report is the ultimate compilation of essential tech statistics. If India's curve follows the same pattern, get ready for big things in the next five years. One thing India is lacking is the catalyst companies that will lead its Internet revolution.
