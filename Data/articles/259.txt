Tesla posts 9.4 percent fall in quarterly deliveries: Tesla Motors said on Tuesday fourth-quarter deliveries fell 9.4 percent due to short-term production hurdles from the transition to a new autopilot hardware. Deliveries fell to about 22,200 vehicles in the fourth quarter from 24,500 vehicles in the preceding quarter. Total deliveries for 2016 of 76,230 also fell short of the company's projection of 80,000 to 90,000. Shares of the company, led by entrepreneur Elon Musk, were down nearly 2 percent at $212.90 in extended trading.
